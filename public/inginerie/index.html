About us

What we do

We offer services for standard and customized applications and projects

Safety

Safety is a valuable asset. At Grivin House, we have made it our duty to develop systems and solutions for people to increase their safety, thereby making the world a safer place. Our products represent the highest level of technology.

Automation

Building Automation means a distributed network of electronic devices designed to monitor and control the mechanical installations, security, fire and flood safety, lighting, humidity control and ventilation systems in a building or group of buildings.

Security

Our easy-to-use alarm systems integrate seamlessly with video cameras, smoke alarms, home automation devices, and more. We offer solutions to address the challenges you face every day.

Communication

Our conference solutions are used by all kinds of customers, ranging from small-sized organizations to large corporations as well as local, regional and even national governments.

Other

We also provide services in the following domains: Architecture, Restorations, Civil engineering, Reinforcements, etc.
